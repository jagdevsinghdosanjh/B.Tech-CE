A matrix \( A \) is symmetric if: \[ A^T = A \]

This means the matrix is equal to its own transpose.

A matrix \( A \) is skew-symmetric if: \[ A^T = -A \]

A square matrix \( Q \) is orthogonal if: \[ Q^T Q = Q Q^T = I \]
